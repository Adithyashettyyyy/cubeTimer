TIMER CONTROLS

    Start/Stop/Reset:

  • The space key to initiate the timer (Start).
  • Press the space key again to halt the timer (Stop).
  • To reset the timer, press the space key once more.

    +2sec Penalty:

  • To ensure fair competition,the +2sec penalty discourages inefficient solving techniques,promoting accuracy and precision
  • Apply this penalty when a solve exceeds the standard solving time due to extra moves or mistakes.

    DNF (Did Not Finish):

  • DNF is used to mark solves that are incomplete or deviate from the standard solving process,maintaining integrity in solving records.
  • Trigger the DNF function when the solve cannot be completed successfully or if the solving conditions are compromised.

    Shuffle Function:

  • Press the "Shuffle" button to receive a randomized cube scramble for your next solve.
  • Alternatively,users can input their own custom scramble if they prefer.
